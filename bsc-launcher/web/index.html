Frequently asked questions

Do I have full ownership of the token?

Yes. Tokens deploy from your connected wallet. You control the contract as the deployer and initial supply recipient.

How can I verify my token on BSCScan?

After deploy, open your token on BSCScan. Verification uses the contract source — contact support for OneX-assisted verification.

How do I show a $1 price on BSCScan?

Use the Liquidity tab to add a USDT pair on PancakeSwap V2 with matching amounts (e.g. 10,000 tokens + 10,000 USDT).
